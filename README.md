# Articles

A home for my opinions and thought pieces on subjects relating to my field —
software engineering, product, and the craft of building things.

This repo is where I collect longer-form writing: essays, reflections, and
arguments worth keeping around. It's less a blog and more a workshop.

## Contents

- **Sustaining the Product Engineer** — on the role of the product engineer and
  what it takes to keep doing the work well.
- **Where the Artefacts Live** — on how writing in AI-assisted engineering now
  serves four distinct functions (context, handoff, evidence, reference) across
  multiple organisational levels, and why where each artefact lives depends on
  both its function and its level.
- **The Team That No Longer Fits** — on why the standard engineering team was
  built around the scarcity of code production, and how that structure should
  change now that AI tooling has made production cheap.
- **The Bottleneck Moved** — on why code review has become the real constraint
  in AI-accelerated engineering, and why the single-reviewer model needs to be
  redesigned rather than asked to read faster.
